# Code Of Conduct

Nagarik Signal is civic infrastructure work. Keep discussion focused, factual, and respectful.

Expected behavior:

- Treat contributors and users with respect.
- Criticize code and claims, not people.
- Keep safety, privacy, and public-interest impact central.
- Be precise about what the MVP proves and what it does not prove.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Adding flows that encourage personal accusations.
- Pushing token, reward, payment, or political attack mechanics into the MVP.

Maintainers may remove issues, comments, or contributions that violate these boundaries.
