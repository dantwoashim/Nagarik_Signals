# Product Landscape

| Product | Primary job | Nagarik Signal boundary |
|---|---|---|
| [Hello Sarkar](https://gunaso.opmcm.gov.np/) | Federal grievance intake and tracking | Independent public evidence record before or beside the official ticket |
| [Kathmandu Gunaso](https://gunaso.kathmandu.gov.np/) | Municipal grievance routing and status | Public hash verification and source-expiring civic memory |
| [Connect KMC](https://apps.apple.com/np/app/connect-kmc/id6767496815) | Official KMC maps, addresses, emergencies, and Public Eye reports | Narrow proof layer that does not require municipal control of the public record |
| [Local Pulse Nepal](https://localpulsenepal.com/) | City ratings, issue map, and affected signals | Delivered-byte verification, provenance classes, and auditable status proof |
| [DevTrack](https://www.devtrack.org/) | Projects, officials, and public accountability forum | Infrastructure evidence commitments without comments or people-focused accusations |
| FixMyStreet / SeeClickFix | Mature issue reporting and service routing | Nepal-focused proof and official-channel handoff |
| Ushahidi | Flexible crowdsourced mapping | Narrower safety and proof contract for public infrastructure |

Nagarik Signal should not compete on the number of pins, votes, or categories. Its useful distinction is a record whose origin, current review window, delivered evidence bytes, and chain commitments can be checked independently.
