# Privacy And Safety

The product is designed to avoid turning civic reporting into personal
accusation or surveillance.

- Public infrastructure only.
- No faces or license plates.
- Approximate location only.
- EXIF stripping before storage.
- Steward moderation for unsafe media.
- No comments.
- No named-official accusation flows.

Immutable proof and mutable display are separated. A proof record can remain
public while harmful media display is hidden.
