# Product FAQ

## Why blockchain?

The product is about public memory. Solana gives public timestamps, committed
evidence hashes, duplicate-prevention PDAs, and a status history that can be
verified outside the operator database.

## Is this a government system?

No. It is a civic proof layer that can work before official adoption.

## Is it Sybil-proof?

Not fully in the MVP. Verification is duplicate-resistant per session or wallet,
not proof-of-personhood.

## Is this mainnet ready?

No. The MVP is devnet-first and needs external security, moderation, legal, and
operations review before production use.
