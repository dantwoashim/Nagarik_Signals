## Submission Text

Nagarik Signal is a Solana-backed public proof layer for ignored civic issues in Nepal. Citizens report safe public infrastructure problems, evidence and metadata are hashed, Issue PDAs anchor the proof, other citizens verify through Verification PDAs, and stewards update status through StatusUpdate PDAs. The dashboard turns those proof objects into ward/locality accountability data.

Competitors collect complaints. Nagarik Signal creates public civic proof and an accountability timeline. Janamat shows what citizens think. Nagarik Signal shows what citizens proved.
